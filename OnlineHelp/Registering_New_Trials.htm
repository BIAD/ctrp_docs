Registering New Trials

This page tree describes how to register new National, Externally Peer-Reviewed, and Institutional trials (collectively referred to as Complete trials) in the CTRP system, and how to import Industrial trials from ClinicalTrials.gov for registration in the CTRP. It applies to both interventional and non-interventional trials.
